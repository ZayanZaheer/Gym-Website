How to Start Bulking?

When starting a bulk, the first step is to determine your maintainace calories - the number of calories you need to maintain your weight.
various online calculators can help you estimate the number.
You then tack on a 10-20% calorie surplus

Weigh yourself regularly to track your progress, shooting for a weight gain of 0.25-0.5% of your body weight per week. A bulking phase can last anywhere from 1 month to over 6 months
