How to Start Cutting?

To Start a cut, its always helpful to determine your maintainace calorie, or how many calories you need to eat per day to maintain your weight


To start a cut, it’s also helpful to determine your maintenance calories, or how many calories you need to eat per day to maintain your weight.
From there, instead of adding calories, you’ll subtract a given number of calories each day. In other words, you’ll eat fewer calories than you need to maintain your body weight.
This will help stimulate fat loss.
