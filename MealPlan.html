How do you like it?

Cutting

A cut is a period of eating at a calorie deficit to lose body fat while maintaining as much muscle mass as possible. Bodybuilders and athletes often implement a cutting phase following a period of bulking.

Bulking

To gain muscle and strength, athletes and recreational gym-goers go through phases called bulking and cutting. These are strategic periods of weight gain and loss, respectively.
